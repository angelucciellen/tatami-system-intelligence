# KNOWLEDGE MODEL

## Version 1.0

*"Knowledge is the memory of intelligence."*

---

# Purpose

The Knowledge Model defines how TATAMI transforms observations into permanent intelligence.

Its purpose is to ensure that every validated discovery becomes reusable knowledge rather than remaining isolated information.

Knowledge is the foundation upon which every future classification, explanation, and research discovery is built.

---

# Levels of Knowledge

TATAMI recognizes five levels.

---

## Level 1 — Observation

An observed event.

Example:

A Trap Resonance occurred during London.

Observations contain no conclusions.

---

## Level 2 — Hypothesis

A possible explanation.

Example:

Trap Resonance appears more frequently during Expansion.

Hypotheses require testing.

---

## Level 3 — Validation

Research confirms statistical evidence.

Example:

Trap Resonance increased execution failure by 64% across 300 events.

Validation creates confidence.

---

## Level 4 — Knowledge

Validated discoveries become permanent.

Knowledge can now influence The Mechanism and Renaissance.

---

## Level 5 — Intelligence

Multiple pieces of knowledge combine to explain complex market behavior.

Intelligence always combines multiple knowledge objects.
