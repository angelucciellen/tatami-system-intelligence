# TATAMI OS Roadmap

# Vision

TATAMI OS is being developed as a Behavioral Market Intelligence Platform.

Rather than focusing on prediction, TATAMI continuously expands its ability to observe, classify, remember, and understand market behavior.

Each phase represents an increase in intelligence—not simply an increase in software features.
