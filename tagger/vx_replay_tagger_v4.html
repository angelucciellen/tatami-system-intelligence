Tag definitions reference

LED — Engine signaled before the move; price followed within 6 bars by ≥ 1 ATR.

CONFIRMED — Engine fired during/after move; useful as confirmation but not predictive. Default for COMPRESSION and DRIFT states unless a break follows.

LAGGED — Engine fired after the move was substantially complete (≥ 80%).

WRONG — Price moved opposite by ≥ 0.5 ATR, or no meaningful move at all.

Honesty check: if you're between two tags, pick the worse one.
